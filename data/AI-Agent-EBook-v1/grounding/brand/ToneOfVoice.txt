BRIDGE PARTNERS — TONE OF VOICE GUIDE
======================================

CORE PERSONALITY
----------------
Bridge Partners sounds like a trusted senior colleague — not a vendor, not a
consultant reciting a slide deck. We are direct, warm, and substantive.

THE THREE PILLARS OF OUR VOICE

1. CONFIDENT — NOT ARROGANT
We state positions clearly. We don't hedge with "it could be argued" or
"some might say." We say what we believe and back it up.
  ✓ "The most effective AI governance programs start with use case selection."
  ✗ "One approach that some organizations have found useful may be to consider..."

2. HUMAN — NOT CORPORATE
We write the way a smart person talks. Contractions are fine. Short sentences
are fine. Starting a sentence with "And" or "But" is fine.
  ✓ "It doesn't have to be complicated. Start with one use case."
  ✗ "It is not necessary to implement a complex solution at the outset."

3. PRACTICAL — NOT THEORETICAL
Every point we make should connect to a real action the reader can take.
If we can't say "so what do I do with this," cut it or rewrite it.
  ✓ "Run a 2-hour data audit before your next AI vendor call. Use our template."
  ✗ "Data quality is an important consideration in AI implementation contexts."

WHAT WE NEVER SOUND LIKE
-------------------------
- A press release: no passive voice, no "pleased to announce"
- A legal document: no jargon walls, no "pursuant to"
- A motivational poster: no "unlock your potential," no "transform your business"
- A textbook: no definitions nobody asked for, no history lessons without payoff

READING LEVEL TARGET
--------------------
Grade 10–12 reading level. Use the Hemingway App to check.
Short words beat long words. Short sentences beat long sentences.
